Overwatch is a beloved first person shooter experience that fans have been waiting for years to be able to enjoy on-the-go with the Nintendo Switch. 2019 finally saw its release and now you can play this action packed Overwatch Nintendo Switch Edition team shooter with friends and family all over the world, wherever you are in the world!

This game truly is a team effort. You and your team choose your player from a roster of 30 characters or "heroes". Each hero has a sppecific role such as tank, damage, and support. Tanks provide protection and have strong weapons. Damage heroes seek out and destroy with a muktitude of weapons and abilities. Support are healers who can offer health to their teammates. All characters can fight but when you work togerther as a team within your roles, you can really dominate a round.

This game requires teamwork and communication to be able to successfully defeat your enemies. It can get a little intense at times but this is one of my favorite games to come along to the Switch console for multi-player enjoyment in a while.
