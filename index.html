Games About

Video games have had a huge influence on me my whole life. Playing them with my friends were some of my happiest memories as a little kid. Since moving to Los Angeles, I lost contact with many of my old friends as distance and time forced us apart. It's a sad truth of growing up and venturing out on your own...but it doesn't have to be.

I've since reconnected with many of those old friends through our love of gaming. That is what sparked the creation of this site. There are many ways to connect with friends and family and for me, playing games and laughing together is one of the joys that I hope to share with you all who have found my site.

Continue into my site to see my current favorite multiplayer Switch games that I use to connect with friends old and new.
